Review ID: Unique identifier for each review.
Author Name: The name of the reviewer.
Review Text: The content of the review.
Star Rating: The rating given by the reviewer (1 to 5 scale).
Helpfulness Count: The number of users who found the review helpful.
Device Type: The device used for the review (e.g., Phone, Tablet, Chromebook).
Date: The date when the review was posted.
